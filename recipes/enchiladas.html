Enchiladas

Baked corn or flower tortillas with a meat filling and served with red chili sauce.

A savory meal originating from Mexico, enchiladas can be enjoyed for dinner or lunch. Some good choices of sides with your enchiladas include black beans, guacamole or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of meat (ground beef or chicken)
  • 12 corn or flour tortillas
  • 2 cans of red enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1 tbsp of diced green chili
  • 1/3 cup of mild salsa

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cook meat at medium heat untl browned
  3. Turn heat down to low, add 1 can of enchilada sauce and cheese. Simmer for 5 minutes on low, stirring occasionally.
  4. Spoon filling into tortillas and place in baking dish.
  5. Cover enchiladas in baking dish with 2nd can of enchilada sauce
  6. Place green chilis and salsa atop enchiladas
  7. Bake enchiladas in oven for 25 minutes
  8. Remove from oven and let stand for 5 minutes before serving
