Air fryer Burgers

Description

Easy burgers made in 20 minutes using an air fryer. French fries or kettle chips make a good side with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • hamburger seasoning
  • sliced cheese
  • hamburger buns
  • condiments and toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. preheat air fryer to 375 degrees
  2. handform 1lb of ground beef into four 1/4lb patties
  3. season both sides with hamburger seasoning
  4. place patties inside preheated air fryer
  5. cook for a total of 20 minutes, flipping patties after 10 minutes
  6. place cheese on patties during last minute of baking
  7. let sit for 5 minutes
  8. place patties on buns, add condiments and serve
