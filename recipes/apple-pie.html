Apple Pie

Description

Simple yet delicious apple pie recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 medium-sized granny smith apples
  • premade pie crust
  • 3 tbps of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 2 tsps of cinnamon

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. peel and core apples into 1/4in slices
  3. mix apples, cinnamon, and sugar in a bowl
  4. layout bottom of pie crust into pie dish
  5. place apple pie filling into pie dish
  6. cut butter into pieces and place around pie filling
  7. layout tope of pie crust over pie filling, seal bottom crust to top crust by pinching it
  8. seperate egg yolk from white and mix with a tbsp of water
  9. brush top of pie crust with egg white
  10. poke 4 small vent holes in pie crust
  11. bake for 15 minutes at 450 degrees, then reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake for 30 minutes
  12. cool for 10 minutes before serving
